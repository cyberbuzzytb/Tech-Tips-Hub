Windows 11 Tips & Tricks

  • Press Win + Z to access Snap Layouts.
  • Use Win + H for voice typing.
  • Tabbed File Explorer lets you manage multiple folders easily.
  • Enable Dark Mode from Settings > Personalization > Colors.
  • Press Win + V to see Clipboard History.
  • Check Widgets with Win + W for news, weather, and calendar.
  • Dynamic Refresh Rate improves battery life on high-refresh displays.
  • Use Win + Ctrl + D to create a new Virtual Desktop.
  • Focus Mode minimizes distractions in Settings > Focus.
  • Right-click the taskbar to customize it.
  • Live Captions help with real-time subtitles in videos.
  • Bluetooth Quick Pair makes connecting devices faster.
  • Use DirectStorage for faster game loading.
  • Storage Sense automates disk cleanup.
  • Press Win + . to quickly open the Emoji Panel.
  • Use Alt + Tab to switch between apps smoothly.
  • Night Light reduces blue light for better sleep.
  • OneDrive Sync automatically backs up your files.
  • Manage Startup Apps from the Task Manager.
